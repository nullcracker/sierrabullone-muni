History of
Sierra Bullones

Old folks said that the name Sierra Bullones was taken from the Spanish words “Sierra” meaning mountain and “Bullones” meaning billions of gold. During the Spanish colonization, wealthy Spaniards were said to have buried billions of gold somewhere between the barangays of Dusita and Canta-ub.
